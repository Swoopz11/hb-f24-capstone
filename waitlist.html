The Waitlist

Have you decided that you are interested in dating the one and only Houston? Well, get in line. Literally. You may add yourself to the waiting list here. Claw your way to the top, but be weary, others may remove you from the list just as fast. Good luck, may the best candidate emerge victorious, and have fun on your date with Houston!
